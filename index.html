About Me

Hi! I'm Aryan Patil, a graduate student at Cornell University - Cornell Tech specializing in Information Systems with a focus on Health Tech. I am deeply passionate about leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML), and AI to build impactful solutions in healthcare. My diverse experiences span startups, large corporations, research, and teaching, where I've worked on projects that improve healthcare accessibility, develop AI-powered systems, and advance NLP for low-resource languages. I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and healthcare innovation to make meaningful social impact.

Additional Information

Click to read more about my journey in NLP, ML, and Health Tech

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I've explored the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML), and health tech. My work includes developing generative AI solutions for knowledge management at ADP, improving low-resource language models at L3Cube, and advancing healthcare accessibility through geospatial data science at ASAR.

One of my most exciting projects involved building a Generative AI Chatbot with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) using AWS Bedrock and Langchain. This system allows efficient querying of PDF documents by creating vector embeddings, enabling real-time contextual responses without the need for extensive fine-tuning.

Additionally, my passion for health tech led me to contribute to high-impact research presented at CUGH and APHA, focusing on quantifying healthcare disparities using geospatial analyses and econometric models.
